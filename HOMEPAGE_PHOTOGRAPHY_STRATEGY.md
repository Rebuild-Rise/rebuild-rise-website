# Homepage Photography Strategy

Status: research pool only. No external photograph has been downloaded, approved, or published.

## Ethical use rule

Archive photography may document the founders' 2022 Abuja relief work only. External photography—even when it documents a real Nigerian project—remains representative context and may not be presented as Rebuild & Rise activity, participants, partners, Pilot 001, or impact. Pexels permits broad website use and modification, but release visibility and sensitive-context consent still require confirmation. Commission-grade work requires written licensing and any client/participant clearance named below.

Direction tags: **TM** Three Movements · **AC** Abuja Chapter · **UC** Under the Canopy · **QD** Quiet Declaration · **HB** Hands Before Claims · **CL** Civic Ledger.

## Acquisition recommendation

Commission an Abuja-based Nigerian documentary photographer for a consented Rebuild & Rise-specific listening and working shoot. Stock can responsibly supply tools, streets, and secondary representative context, but the production homepage's opening human record should belong truthfully to the organization’s own story and consent framework.
